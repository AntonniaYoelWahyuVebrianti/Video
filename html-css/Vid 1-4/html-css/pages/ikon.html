iKON

iKON is a South Korean boy group formed by YG Entertainment, officially debuting on September 15, 2015. Known for their dynamic performance and charismatic presence, iKON has made a significant impact in the K-pop industry with heir diverse music and engaging stage shows. The group originally debuted with seven members but currently sonsists of six.

iKON debuted with their mini-album Welcome Back, featuring hits like "My Type" and "Rhythm Ta". These tracks quickly gained popularity, establishing iKON as prominent name in the Korean music scene. Prior to their officially debut, the member participated in survival programs such as Show Me the Money 3, which showcased their talents and build anticipation for their debut.

iKON has released several successful albums, including Retrun (2018) and I DECIDE (2020). Their songs such as "Love Scenario", "Killing Me", And "Why Why Why", have become major hits, earning them recognition on various music charts and awards.

iKON has played a significant role n popularizing K-pop globally. Their music often exsplores themes of love, struggle, and motivation, resonating with fans across diferent backgrounds. They are also actively involved in social and charitable activities, demonstrating their commitment to their comunity and fans.

iKON continues to achieve success with their consistent activities and dedication to delivering innovative music and performance,maintaning their position as a major name in the K-pop industry.
