DOK2

DOK2 (pronounced "Dokki"), born Lee Joon-kyung on March 28, 1990, is a South Korean rapper, songwriter, and record producer. He is widely recognized as a pioneer in the Korean hip-hop scene and has played a significant role in popularizing the genre both in Korea and internationally. Known for his distinctive rap style, charismatic presence, and prolific production work, Dok2 has established himself as one of the most influential figures in Korean hip-hop.

Dok2 was born to a Korean mother and a Filipino-Spanish father, and he began his music career at a young age. Influenced by hip-hop from an early age, he started writing and producing his own music as a teenager. At 13, Dok2 was already making beats and quickly caught the attention of established Korean hip-hop artists.

He first gained recognition as part of the hip-hop duo All Black with rapper Microdot in 2006, though the group disbanded shortly after. Despite the setback, Dok2 continued to pursue his passion for music, building a reputation as a talented young producer and rapper.

Dok2’s big break came when he signed with Epik High's Map the Soul label as a producer, contributing to numerous projects that helped solidify his standing in the industry. In 2009, Dok2 launched his solo career with the release of his first mini-album, Thunderground, showcasing his raw talent and establishing his signature style.

In 2011, Dok2 co-founded Illionaire Records with fellow rapper The Quiett. Illionaire Records quickly became one of the most respected labels in Korean hip-hop, known for its independent ethos and roster of talented artists.

Dok2 is known for his distinctive flow, confident lyrics, and anthemic beats. His music often reflects themes of success, wealth, and personal struggles, resonating with listeners for its authenticity and boldness. Over the years, Dok2 has released numerous albums, singles, and collaborations that have consistently topped charts and earned critical acclaim.

Notable works include:

  • - "We Gotta Know" – A track that established his presence in the hip-hop scene.
  • - "1llin’" – One of his biggest hits, known for its catchy beat and iconic lyrics.
  • - "On My Way" – A collaboration with various artists, showcasing his versatility and broad appeal.
Throughout his career, Dok2 has received numerous awards and accolades for his contributions to music. His work has not only shaped the Korean hip-hop landscape but also inspired a new generation of artists. As a producer and mentor, Dok2 has been instrumental in developing the careers of many up-and-coming rappers and producers.

In 2019, Dok2 joined 143 Entertainment, where he continues to expand his influence and explore new musical directions. His dedication to the craft and unwavering commitment to authenticity have solidified his place as a legend in the industry.

At 143 Entertainment, Dok2 remains active in producing, collaborating, and performing. He is continually exploring new sounds and pushing the boundaries of his artistry. Dok2’s journey reflects his resilience and passion, making him a respected and enduring figure in the world of hip-hop.

Dok2 continues to inspire with his relentless work ethic and creative vision, proving that with determination and talent, boundaries can always be broken.
